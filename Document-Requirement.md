## 2. Website Description

An e-commerce platform for selling furniture, small appliances, and office furniture, serving customers in Paralakhemundi, Gajapathi District, Odisha, and beyond. Sri Matha Furniture has been serving customers across Odisha and Andhra Pradesh for the past 35 years. The website aims to provide a seamless online shopping experience with a futuristic shopping approach.
